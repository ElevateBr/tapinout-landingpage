The Problem with Traditional Time Tracking Systems

Traditional time tracking systems, such as mechanical clocks or manual spreadsheets, present several limitations:

  • Lack of accuracy: Data can be easily altered or lost
  • Manual process: Consumes valuable HR time for calculations and checks
  • Fraud risks: Possibility of "clocking in" by third parties
  • Access difficulty: Information centralized in one physical location
  • Legal compliance: Greater difficulty in meeting labor law requirements
