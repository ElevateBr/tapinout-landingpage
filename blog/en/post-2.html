How TapInOut Meets the Requirements of Regulation 671/2021

August 12, 2025 7 min read Legal Compliance

Regulation 671/2021 brought significant changes to Brazilian labor legislation, especially regarding work schedule control. In this article, we explain how TapInOut ensures total compliance with these requirements, protecting your company from fines and labor lawsuits.

What is Regulation 671/2021?

Ordinance 671/2021, published by the Ministry of Labor and Social Security, establishes new rules for work schedule control, especially regarding remote and hybrid work. The main requirements include:

  • Mandatory schedule control: Even in remote work, it's necessary to record working hours
  • Data security: Information must be protected against alterations
  • Traceability: System must allow complete audit
  • Identity validation: Guarantee that the employee is who they claim to be
  • System integration: Compatibility with other business systems

Important

Companies that don't meet the requirements of regulation 671/2021 can face fines ranging from $200 to $2,000 per employee, plus labor lawsuits that can result in million-dollar judgments.

TapInOut implements a schedule control system that meets all regulation requirements:
